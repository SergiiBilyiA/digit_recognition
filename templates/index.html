About App:

This application can recognize handwritten digits. It is based on a convolutional neural network with eight layers and almost three and a half million parameters. Training and validation was carried out on data collected using this site.
Draw a number in the area above and click the "predict" button to see the prediction. To clear the area, press the "clear" button.
